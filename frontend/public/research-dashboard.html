Investment Thesis

Larsen & Toubro stands as India's most diversified engineering and construction conglomerate, with operations spanning infrastructure, hydrocarbon, defence, heavy engineering, and IT services. The company benefits from a structural multi-decade capex cycle in India driven by government spending on roads, railways, airports, ports, urban infrastructure, and defence indigenisation.

The order book of ₹5.5L Cr (as of Q4FY26) — at 2.3x TTM revenue — provides exceptional earnings visibility through FY28. Order inflows have accelerated to ₹2.8L Cr in FY26 (+22% YoY), reflecting L&T's dominant market positioning in infrastructure project execution. The international order pipeline — now 35% of backlog — diversifies revenue and reduces India-specific risk.

Margin improvement is a key re-rating trigger: EBITDA margins have expanded from 10.8% in FY23 to 12.1% in FY26, with management guiding 13–14% by FY28 as higher-margin international projects and Defence segment scale. With a robust balance sheet (net cash positive at holding company), strong execution capability, and ESG leadership, L&T deserves a premium multiple.
